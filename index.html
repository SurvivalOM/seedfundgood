The three rungs

1. Idea

Post it. Gather believers who press "I'd back this." No money — validation before cash. This is the rung the big platforms refuse to serve.

2. Validation

A small round from early believers — rewards, pre-orders, or donations. Keep what you raise, paid straight to you.

3. Launch

The big all-or-nothing round, earned by delivering your validation promises. Goal met, the escrow releases. Goal missed, every backer is refunded.

Why SeedGood is different

  • We fund the idea stage. Big platforms demand a prototype. Here, a good idea and a trusted person are enough to begin.
  • Trust is public. Every promise a creator makes is logged in an open ledger — kept, late, or broken. Reputation is earned, rung by rung.
  • The money never touches us. Every rupee moves through licensed payment rails from backer to creator. We are the community, not the bank.
  • Good-money screened. Every money round passes five public standards: real value, no exploitation, honest claims, public promises, and support — never investment.
  • Members create free. FPL members and students pay no platform fee, ever. Approved outside creators pay a small honest fee only on success.
Backing on SeedGood is support for a promise — a reward, a pre-order, a membership, or a gift. It is never an investment and never promises any financial return.
